How it works

1

Snap one selfie

A guided full-body capture becomes your avatar. The silhouette is generated on your iPhone with Apple's Vision framework, and you can tune the proportions to match you.

2

Photograph your clothes

Shoot or import each item once. The background is removed on device, and your pieces are organized into tops, bottoms, shoes, and accessories.

3

Mix, match, save

Tap items onto your avatar, drag them into place, and adjust the fit. Save the looks you love to your lookbook and reopen them any morning you're stuck.

Your closet is nobody's data

Everything stays on your iPhone.

  • No account, no cloud, no server.
  • No analytics and no tracking.
  • No photos sent to third-party AI.
  • Your data leaves only in your own encrypted device backups.
