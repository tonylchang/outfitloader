# Purpose

## Problem Statement

Outfitloader is an iOS app that turns a full-body selfie into a customizable digital avatar and lets users photograph their real clothing items to build a virtual closet. Users can drag, drop, and combine tops, bottoms, shoes, and accessories onto their avatar to preview complete outfits on their own body shape and proportions.

The app exists to eliminate the exhausting, time-consuming cycle of physically trying on multiple outfits, rummaging through a closet, and creating a mess while deciding what to wear. It helps users visually experiment with outfit combinations in seconds, reduce decision fatigue, and avoid last-minute wardrobe panic.

## Target Users

Outfitloader is for anyone who wants to streamline their daily dressing routine, especially:

- Busy professionals who need polished outfits without morning trial-and-error.
- Fashion enthusiasts and outfit planners who want to experiment without pulling items off hangers.
- Frequent travelers who want to plan versatile mix-and-match looks before packing.
- People with large or cluttered closets who struggle to remember what they own or feel overwhelmed by choice.
- Sustainability-minded shoppers who want to use their existing wardrobe more effectively before buying more.
- People who dislike changing clothes repeatedly, including users with mobility issues, sensory sensitivities, or a low tolerance for mess.

## Prior Art

Outfitloader builds on digital wardrobe and virtual try-on products such as Fitted, Aesty AI, Drapr, Alta, Indyx, COSH!, Whering, and Acloset. It also improves on manual workflows such as trying on clothes and taking mirror selfies, laying outfits on a bed or floor, saving inspiration in Pinterest or Instagram, and tracking wardrobe items in notes or spreadsheets.

The app should improve on these alternatives through faster digitization, more personalized visualization based on the user's own full-body selfie, and a simpler product focus: a digital closet plus a realistic avatar for quick mix-and-match outfit planning.
